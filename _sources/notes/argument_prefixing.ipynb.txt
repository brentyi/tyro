    "# Argument Prefixing\n",
    "\n",
    "When nesting structures in `tyro`, arguments are prefixed with the parent\n",
    "field name to avoid naming collisions. For example, a field `learning_rate`\n",
    "inside a field called `optimizer` becomes `--optimizer.learning-rate`.\n",
    "\n",
    "This page covers ways to control this prefixing behavior."

    "### `prefix_name=False`: drop the parent's prefix\n",
    "\n",
    "`tyro.conf.arg(prefix_name=False)` on a field means \"don't prefix **me**\n",

    "`prefix_name=False` on `optimizer` drops the `train.` prefix from\n",
